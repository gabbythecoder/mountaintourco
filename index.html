Banff Gondola

Find yourself on the summit of Sulphur Mountain high above the town of Banff. Offering panoramic views of the Canadian Rockies.

Moraine Lake

Located in the Valley of the Ten Peaks, Moraine Lake is a glacially-fed lake in Banff National Park. The lake is known for its vibrant blue-green color.

Takakkaw Falls

Located in Yoho National Park, Takakkaw Falls is the second tallest waterfall in Canada. The falls are fed by the Daly Glacier and are a must-see for any visitor to the park.

Lake Louise

Located in Banff National Park, Lake Louise is a glacial lake known for its turquoise, glacier-fed waters ringed by high peaks. The lake is a popular destination for hiking and canoeing.
